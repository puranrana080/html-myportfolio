Puran Receipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • half cup worm milk
  • 2 half teaspoon yeast
  • half cup granulated suger
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • half cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 and half tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. add in the floor and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. then at last eat everything.
